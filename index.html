    About Us

    The club brought together 40 like-minded students from different streams with the motivation of bringing out new technologies and research in the field of EVs, harnessing and storing the green form of energies.

    Our Mission

    • Emphasising reliablilty on Renewable sources of energy.
    • Implementation of Electric Vehicles as sustainable, reliable and economical mode of transport.
    • Replacement of existing IC engines with induction motor, powererd by LiFePO4

    Our Vision

    • Electrification of Indian Public Transportation.
    • Incorporation of sustainable sources of energy in day-to-day utility.
    • Initiation of Research and Development Centre in the domain of EVs, batteries and their storage capabilities.
